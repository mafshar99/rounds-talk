The road here

How a chatbot became a colleague.

~2012

Neural networks

Machines that learn patterns from data, not rules.

2017

Attention

"Attention Is All You Need." Models learn what matters in context.

2018–’20

Pretrained LMs

BERT, GPT. Language becomes a reusable substrate.

2022

Chatbots & LLMs

ChatGPT. Language becomes conversation, at scale.

2024–now

LLM-driven agents

Language becomes action: planning, tools, follow-through.

Each step added a capacity. The last one changed the posture — from answering to acting.
The definition

A model answers a question.
An agent pursues a goal.

01

Reasoning

Breaks a goal into steps. Plans, checks its work, and re-plans when reality disagrees.

02

Tool calling

Acts on the world — queries the EHR, runs a score, retrieves a guideline, writes a note.

03

Memory

Carries context across the encounter, not just the sentence. Knows what came before.

04

Conversation

Collaborates — with you, and with other agents — toward a shared, supervised outcome.

Put those four together under supervision, and you don't have a tool. You have a teammate.
